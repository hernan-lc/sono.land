Viewer Information

As a Viewer:

  • You will not share your camera/microphone
  • You will only receive video from broadcasters
  • You can watch streams without being seen

As a Broadcaster:

  • You will share your camera/microphone
  • Other participants can see your video
  • You can stream to viewers
